About Us

The number of coronavirus cases in the United States has continued to grow exponentially in the past few months, topping the chart globally. Abiding by the CDC guidelines, most people are wearing face masks, keeping their social distances, and restaurants along with other entertainment facilities have delayed opening. However, the nation’s cases are still soaring. As members of the community, we want to address this concern by bringing in some measures other countries have attempted to efficiently “flatten the curve.” The result of our research and gathering multiple sources of information -- CovidInfo.
